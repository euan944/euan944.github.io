About Us

GCU racing is a society centred around motor racing, the primary objective of the society is to compete in the Formula Students UK (FSUK) competition.
We are currently designing & building our car for 2021, our first challenger for the Class 1 competition. Our goal for this season is to win the Best Newcomer Award.

We may be a small team but we have a lot of firepower and are determined to produce a fast and reliable car.
If this is something you'd like to get involved with then check out our sponsor us tab on our page or you can also donate through our just giving page.
