About Us

We are a group of motivated students studying at Glasgow Caledonian University in Scotland. We are students of engineering, computing, business and marketing.​ GCU Racing is structured with each member being a member of two of our sub-teams, these will consist of 1 R&D Team and 1 Business Team with a couple of exceptions for business students who are involved in more than 1 business team.
Each of these sub-teams has a team leader who feeds directly to their team head. The team head then works closely with the management team & staff liaison from the University to organise the team, provide assistance and manage communication with the University.
